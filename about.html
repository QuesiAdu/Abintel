Who We are

AB Intel Limited was incorporated in Ghana on 21st October, 2002 as a multi-disciplinary consultancy limited liability company under Ghana’s Companies Code 1963 (Act 179). Prior to coming together to form the Company, its Chief Executive and core Consultants have been in industrial practices in various capacities and consultancy business for well over 15 years. Each member of our extraordinary team has success stories of their own and cumulatively we can boast of over 100 years of professional experiences.

Our Mission

AB Intel recognises that continued success could only be assured through total commitment to its Clients. Our commitment is to provide the highest value for money for our Clients by consistently emphasising innovation, operational excellence and the highest quality in everything we do while maintaining the highest professional and ethical standards. In short, a service that not only meets Clients’ needs but also exceeds their expectations.

Our Goal

Our goal is to be recognised as the leading consulting firm in each of our areas of specialisation and to maintain standards of excellence in everything we do that distinguishes us from our competitors.

OUR STRATEGY

Our strategy is to focus on businesses in which our core competence and experience can be brought to bear for maximum return to our Clients
